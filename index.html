Icenomics — NHL Analytics

Open, reproducible, honest hockey analytics: 16 seasons of on-ice data, validated WAR, market value, and GM evaluation. Every number traceable; every caveat named.

Player cards Career WAR arcs, play-driving trends, shot maps, scorecards, and contract value for every rated skater and goalie since 2010. Leaders WAR boards filterable by season range, component (Total / EV / PP / PK), position, and regular season vs playoffs. Projections — standings, playoff & Cup odds Every team's 2026-27 projection from roster WAR — with a live what-if trade simulator: move players between teams and watch the standings and odds re-simulate, each number explained by the underlying play (not the lucky record). Front office — roster toolkit + GM ratings Every team's summer view: roster WAR, contracts, trade values priced in real pick packages, expiring decisions, and the prospect pipeline — plus who drafts, signs, and trades best (slot-adjusted, zero-sum audited). Free agents — UFA market + best fits The 2026-27 UFA market ranked by our value, each with the teams that fit by need and cap space — plus the pending-RFA re-sign board. Draft Board — 2026 class Production-ranked draft eligibles with projected 7-season NHL WAR — a rounds-2-7 value tool that names what it can't see (skating, sense, NCAA), not a top-10 scouting list. Transactions, graded Every trade and signing in the league, scored the moment it lands — trades by the WAR exchanged, signings by production value vs the cap hit. A live, self-scoring ledger. Reliability — how to read these numbers The honesty page: what r ≈ 0.85 means, per-metric calibration reports, and the limits we name instead of hide.
