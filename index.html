Receipts on every numberTap any grade on any card — the method, the sample, and the caveats open right there.
Validated before shippedModel changes pass pre-registered gates or they don't land — and the refusals are published, not hidden.
Honest ceilingsEvery metric ships with what it can't see — playoffs, thin samples, and our own predictive limits, measured and posted.
Player cards Career WAR arcs, play-driving trends, shot maps, scorecards, and contract value for every rated skater and goalie since 2010 — plus the full WAR leaderboards by season range, component, and position, one tap away. Deal Grader — skater contracts reconstructed Eligible multi-year skater deals evaluated in their signing-year age and cap frame using the current model. Outcome-calibrated letters and a transparent receipt behind each grade; goalies are not yet included. Game cards — the latest night Every game with every player's line and shot-quality context — three stars, single-game goalie GSAx, all honestly labeled as one night, never a rating. In season: last night, nightly. Projections — standings, playoff & Cup odds Every team's 2026-27 projection from roster WAR — with a live what-if trade simulator: move players between teams and watch the standings and odds re-simulate, each number explained by the underlying play (not the lucky record). Offseason — who won the summer Every team's final 2025-26 roster vs today: who came in, who walked, every trade and signing graded — the net projected-points swing, ranked league-wide. Front office — roster toolkit + GM ratings Every team's summer view: roster WAR, contracts, trade values priced in real pick packages, expiring decisions, and the prospect pipeline — plus who drafts, signs, and trades best (slot-adjusted, zero-sum audited). Free agents — UFA market + best fits The 2026-27 UFA market ranked by our value, each with the teams that fit by need and cap space — plus the pending-RFA re-sign board. Draft Board — 2026 class Production-ranked draft eligibles with projected 7-season NHL WAR — a rounds-2-7 value tool that names what it can't see (skating, sense, NCAA), not a top-10 scouting list. Transactions, graded Every trade and signing in the league, scored the moment it lands — trades by the WAR exchanged, signings by production value vs the cap hit. A live graded log; historical scoring is published separately only when its evidence is locked. Reliability — how to read these numbers The honesty page: what r ≈ 0.85 means, per-metric calibration reports, and the limits we name instead of hide.

Static snapshot baked from the local warehouse. The type-anything tools (Ask, SQL Lab, cap optimizer) run in the local build only — the trade simulator on the Projections page runs right here, client-side.
